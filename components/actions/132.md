# 132: Provide Technology Metrics To Teams

**Specification Version:** 0.9.0

**Slug:** 132-provide-metrics

**Implementation Types:**

## Descriptive Statement

Provide allied teams the technology metrics they need.

## Supplemental Guidance

Example is license-impacted spend. While some data is shared, other data is siloed but would be useful to share.

## Scoring Guide

**Weight:** 1.0

### Formula

* Share relevant data already maintained and tracked by FinOps
* Share metric reporting and the calculations behind FinOps created metrics
* Create new metrics from FinOps and other data based on needs of allied teams
