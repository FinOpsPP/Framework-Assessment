## Descriptive Statement

Use shared influence to drive adoption and resolve conflicts quickly.

## Supplemental Guidance

An example of shared influence is removing idle instances due to both cost and security vulnerabilities

## Scoring Guide

### Formula

* Come up with shared responses as allied teams to external conflicts
* Resolve internal conflicts before deciding on the next steps needed to reach shared goals
* Communication to external teams or leaders includes the full story of how allied teams achieve goals together
