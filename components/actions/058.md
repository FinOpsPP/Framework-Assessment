# 58: Set Review Cadence & Targets

**Specification Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Accepted

**Slug:** 58-set-cadence

**Implementation Types:**

* None

## Descriptive Statement

Set review cadence; align targets with product and finance goals.

## Supplemental Guidance

Metric changes could be due to changes in usage, rates, or even input definitions or data integrity issues.
Reviews are important to capture changes in the business, but also mistakes in the data ingestion process.

## Scoring Guide

**Weight:** 1.0

### Formula

* Collaborate with stakeholders on desired acceptable thresholds or goals for each unit economic value
* Review data with stakeholders when metrics exceed acceptable thresholds in either direction
* Incorporate metric reviews into the decision making processes of stakeholder departments
* The FinOps team should periodically publish an analysis of major changes in the metrics dashboards
  as well as facilitate reviews of metrics, adoption data, modifications, and KPI refreshes
